Helsinki open device lab

Visit the lab

Open device lab is established to help the Helsinki web developer community to be able to test their responsive designs on a growing range of mobile devices.

About the project

The web doesn’t live on a desk anymore and we now need to test our work on multiple devices, instead of just few desktop browsers. Read more

Visit the lab

The idea is that anyone can pop round and use the devices. We are located downtown Helsinki, Between Kamppi and Ruoholahti. View location

Participate in the project

Anyone can participate and help the developer community of Helsinki by lending or donating their old devices to the lab. Participate
